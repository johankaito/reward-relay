## WHAT IS REWARDIFY?

Reward Relay automates credit card churning for Australians. We track your cards, remind you when to cancel, recommend which card to apply for next, and optimize every dollar you spend.

**The Problem**:
Australian churners manually track credit cards in spreadsheets, forget optimal cancel dates, and miss opportunities. Zero automated tools exist in the AU market.

**The Solution**:
Automated tracking, intelligent recommendations, and optimizationâ€”eliminating the manual spreadsheet work.

**Why This Exists**:
Founder (John Keto) is a churner who got tired of manual tracking and built this for himself. If it solves his problem, it solves the problem for 100,000 other Australian churners.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Why Reward Relay vs just using PointsHacks?
**A**: PointsHacks is content/education. Reward Relay is automation/execution. They're complementary (we may partner).

### Q: Why would people pay $49/month for this?
**A**: Time savings (40 hours/year) + optimized earnings (50K+ extra points = $500-1,000 value) = ROI of 10:1.

### Q: What if banks kill churning entirely?
**A**: Unlikely (banks profit from churners who miss cancel dates). But if they do, pivot to general reward optimization (broader market).

### Q: Can you reach $1M/month in Australia?
**A**: Noâ€”AU market caps at ~$50K MRR. Would need US/UK expansion. But $50K MRR = $600K ARR = profitable business + exit opportunity.

### Q: Why not build mobile app first?
**A**: Web-first is faster to build and iterate. Can add mobile later if traction proven.

### Q: What about bank account integration?
**A**: Too risky (legal, security) and slow to build for MVP. Manual entry + CSV upload is sufficient for validation.
