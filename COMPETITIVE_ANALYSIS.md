## MARKET ENTRY BARRIERS (Why Competition Won't Come Soon)

### Barrier 1: Market Appears Too Small
- 100K users looks small to VCs
- Not "venture scale" (kills VC-backed competitors)
- Perfect for solo founder, unattractive to funded startups

### Barrier 2: Complex Domain Knowledge
- Requires understanding of credit card churning
- Bank-specific rules are nuanced
- AU-specific knowledge required
- High learning curve for generalists

### Barrier 3: Community Trust Required
- Churners are skeptical of corporate tools
- Requires authentic community participation
- Can't buy trust, must earn it
- Takes 6-12 months to build

### Barrier 4: Data Collection Time
- Bank blacklisting patterns require months/years of data
- Approval/rejection data points from community
- Spending optimization requires user data
- Can't accelerate data collection with capital
